OUR STORY

We are Building the Home of Fashion in North Cyprus.

Bomnous was born from a simple observation — incredible fashion exists in North Cyprus, but discovering it was harder than it should be. Local boutiques, independent sellers, and African diaspora brands had no shared space to be found, celebrated, and shopped.

We built Bomnous to change that. A curated fashion marketplace where independent sellers get visibility, buyers discover pieces they actually love, and AI helps match every person to their perfect style — regardless of budget, occasion, or background.

OUR MISSION

Smart Shopping. Real Sellers. Global Style.

Bomnous is not just a marketplace — it’s a fashion community. We celebrate European elegance, African cultural richness, and North Cyprus local style all in one intelligent platform. Every seller on Bomnous is verified, every product is curated, and every buyer deserves a premium experience.
